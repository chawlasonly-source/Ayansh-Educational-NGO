Our Education Initiatives

Shaping minds, building futures, and sparking the ray of knowledge.

📚 Free Remedial Classes

Many children in underserved communities go to school but fall behind due to a lack of guidance at home. Ayansh conducts daily after-school learning centers where children get help with homework, clear their core concepts, and learn through interactive activities.

🎒 Distribution Drives

Financial barriers shouldn't stop a child from studying. We actively collect and distribute basic necessities like school bags, textbooks, stationary kits, and uniforms to families who struggle to afford basic school supplies.
